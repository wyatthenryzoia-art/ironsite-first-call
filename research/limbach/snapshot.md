# Limbach Holdings · Snapshot

## Facts

- **Ticker:** NASDAQ: LMB
- **HQ:** Warrendale, PA (Pittsburgh area)
- **Founded:** 1901 (as the Limbach Company)
- **Ownership:** Public, listed on NASDAQ
- **FY2024 revenue:** $518.8M (+0.5% YoY) [Mar 10, 2025 press release]
- **FY2025 guidance (Q3 update):** $650-680M revenue; $80-86M adjusted EBITDA
- **FY2024 adjusted EBITDA:** $63.7M (+36.1%)
- **FY2024 net income:** $30.9M (+48.8%)
- **FY2024 gross margin:** 27.8% (up from 23.1%)
- **Team members:** ~1,400
- **Offices:** 20 across the eastern United States
- **Verticals (6):** healthcare, industrial and manufacturing, data centers, life science, higher education, cultural and entertainment
- **Business model:** ODR (Owner Direct Relationships) vs. GCR (General Contractor Relationships). ODR was 76.6% of Q3 2025 revenue.
- **Craft workforce:** majority covered by collective bargaining agreements (per 10-K)

## Recent acquisitions (2024-2025)

- **Pioneer Power** · Jul 1, 2025 · $66.1M cash · industrial mechanical, Upper Midwest, healthcare/food/power/utility verticals. Expected to add $120M annualized revenue and $10M EBITDA starting 2026.
- **Consolidated Mechanical (CMI)** · 2024 · $23M · mechanical, millwright, steel fab, plumbing; KY/MI/IL.
- **ACME Industrial Piping** and **Industrial Air** · 2024 · expanding industrial footprint.

## Notable projects / customer examples (from Q3 2025 call)

- Healthcare facility assessment: national healthcare owner engaged Limbach across 20 locations; initiative translated to $12M in capital projects at four sites in Q3 2025.
- Data center and industrial verticals named as primary growth targets in Jan 2026 executive realignment press release.

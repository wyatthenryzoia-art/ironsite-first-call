**Date:** Nov 27, 2025

**Source:** https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/27/limbach-lmb-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/

**Supporting quotes:**
- "Approximately one-third of ODR revenue comes from quick-burning work such as maintenance contracts, time and material, and work orders."

**Grade:** Strong. Direct transcript from CEO/CFO on a live earnings call.

**Date:** Jul 1, 2025

**Source:** https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250701595283/en/Limbach-Acquires-Pioneer-Power

**Supporting facts:**
- Purchase price: $66.1M cash.
- Pioneer Power (est. 1947) serves healthcare, food, power/utility, oil refining verticals.
- Adds ~$120M annualized revenue and ~$10M adjusted EBITDA starting 2026.
- Follows Consolidated Mechanical ($23M, 2024) and earlier ACME Industrial Piping / Industrial Air acquisitions.

**Connection to Ironsite's wedge:** Four industrial mechanical acquisitions inside 18 months means four different field-ops cultures, tool stacks, and craft union locals. Standardized per-worker productivity data is the fastest way to normalize output across the newly combined companies without a long process-integration project. Wearable rollout is faster than an ERP migration.

**Grade:** Strong. Company press release with financial terms disclosed.
